General Info

My name is Max Cohen and I am an aspiring Sofware Engineer/Developer. I dabbled a bit with coding starting from middle school, but I really started to learn get into good Computer Science practices during senior year of high school.
I have worked as the Database and Internal Website Manager of Fass Selections for the past 6 years, and I also was a Software Development Intern this past summer.
I am currently a second year student at UCSD studying Computer Science.
